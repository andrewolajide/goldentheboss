Frequently Asked Questions

How long do training programs typically take?

Individual coaching sessions are 2 hours each. Team training workshops are full-day intensives (8 hours). Organization programs vary from 2-5 days depending on your needs and goals.

Do you offer virtual training sessions?

Yes, all training programs are available both in-person and virtually via video conference. Virtual sessions are just as effective and allow for flexible scheduling.

What's included in the follow-up support?

All training programs include email support, resource materials, action plan templates, and scheduled check-ins to ensure you're implementing what you've learned successfully.

Can I customize a training program for my specific needs?

Absolutely! All programs can be customized based on your experience level, market conditions, and specific challenges. We'll discuss your goals during the initial consultation.
